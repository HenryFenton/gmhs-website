Welcome to Garvey Maceo High School

Garvey Maceo High School (G.M.H.S) opened its doors on May 19, 1980, a gift from the government and people of Cuba, fittingly named after Jamaican national hero Marcus Garvey and Cuban revolutionary Antonio Maceo. Originally built as a Comprehensive High School with a major focus on agriculture, GMHS owns 90 acres of land and operates a commercial farm as well as a tutorial farm, engaging students in both vocational and academic subject areas.
