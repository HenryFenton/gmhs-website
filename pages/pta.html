What We Do

The PTA plays a vital role in building a thriving school community for students, parents and staff

🤝

Build Relationships

We foster harmonious and meaningful relationships between parents and staff, ensuring open communication and mutual respect throughout the school year.

🏫

Enhance Campus Life

From fundraising to school events, the PTA performs important functions that directly improve the quality of campus life for every student and teacher.

📋

School Governance

The sitting PTA President serves on the Board of Management, giving parents a direct voice in school policy and strategic decision-making.

💡

Support Student Success

We advocate for the academic, social and emotional needs of students — working alongside teachers to ensure every child has the support they need to thrive.

Meeting Schedule

The PTA meets at least three times per year and all parents and guardians are welcome to attend

Sep 20

Term 1 General Meeting

Opening meeting of the academic year. Review of school priorities, introduction of new executive members and planning for the term ahead.

6:00 PM @ School Hall
Jan 18

Term 2 General Meeting

Mid-year review of student progress, school improvement updates and community fundraising initiatives for the spring term.

6:00 PM @ School Hall
Apr 26

Term 3 Annual General Meeting

Year-end review, election of new executive members, financial report and planning for the upcoming academic year.

6:00 PM @ School Hall
