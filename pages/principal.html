Principal's Message
2026 / 2027

A word from our school leader

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world."
Embrace your motto: Excelencia en estudio, trabajo y recreo.
Mrs. Erica Ewbanks Principal

At the forefront of education in Jamaica, Garvey Maceo High School stands as a proud institution committed to academic excellence, character development, and the holistic growth of every student. Rooted in a rich legacy and guided by a progressive vision, we strive to equip our students with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to thrive in an ever-evolving global society.

Our mission is to empower each student to achieve their fullest potential, academically, socially, and personally, while fostering resilience, discipline, and a strong sense of purpose. We believe that while school is hard work, it is never impossible. The challenges our students encounter are not barriers, but stepping stones to greater achievement. We encourage each learner to embrace their uniqueness, set meaningful goals, and work diligently to realise them, becoming beacons of inspiration for others.

At Garvey Maceo High School, education extends beyond the classroom. While academic success is a priority, we place equal importance on developing leadership, teamwork, confidence, and integrity. Guided by our motto, 'Excellence in work, study and play' we celebrate achievement in all its forms. We recognise that greatness is not limited to academics alone but our students also excel in sports and other talents, building pathways to success and lifelong opportunities.

As we look ahead, we remain steadfast in our commitment to excellence in every area of school life. Together, we will make each year one of growth, achievement, and lasting impact.

🔍

Curiosity

Being open to other ways of seeing the world and exploring new ideas with enthusiasm.

🎯

Personal Accountability

Staying disciplined in decision-making, proactive in correcting mistakes and self-motivated in our studies.

💡

Critical Thinking

Gathering and objectively evaluating information through reflection, analysis and reason.

As we embark on this new academic year, I encourage every student to push beyond perceived limits. Greatness is not reserved for the few, it belongs to those who dare to work for it. Our teachers, staff and community stand beside you every step of the way.

Let us build a year defined by excellence, compassion and achievement for ourselves, our families and our school.

Mrs. Erica Ewbanks Principal, Garvey Maceo High School
