How to Access Your Resources

Follow these simple steps to find your curriculum guides and log in to your online learning tools

1
📋

Find Your Form

Scroll to the Curriculum section below and select the card matching your form group 1st to 3rd, 4th & 5th or 6th Form.

2
⬇️

Download Your Guide

Click the gold Download button next to your subject or document. Guides are in PDF format, open them on any device.

3
🔑

Get Your Access Code

Scroll to the Access Codes section. Your LMS and Zoom codes are distributed by your form teacher at the start of each term.

4
🖥️

Log In & Learn

Use the Quick Access bar above to jump straight to the LMS, Zoom or Library. If you have trouble, contact the school office.
