Vice Principal's Message
2026 / 2027

A word from senior leadership

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world."
Embrace your motto: Excelencia en estudio, trabajo y recreo.
Ms. Dian Fenton -Thompson Vice Principal

At Garvey Maceo High School, our focus is not only on what students learn, but on who they become throughout their journey with us. As Vice Principal, I take great pride in helping to cultivate a school culture where students are encouraged to think critically, act responsibly, and grow into confident, capable individuals.

Each day presents new opportunities for growth, and we challenge our students to approach their education with determination and curiosity. While the path to success may require effort and resilience, we remind our students that progress comes through consistency and a willingness to learn from every experience. With the right mindset and support, every goal becomes attainable.

Our school is a place where talents are discovered and nurtured. Whether in the classroom, on the field, or through creative expression, students are encouraged to explore their interests and develop their strengths. We believe that true success lies in balance, and we are committed to guiding our students as they build both academic competence and strong personal values.

Looking ahead, we remain dedicated to providing a safe, supportive, and engaging environment where all students can thrive. By working together as a united school community, we will continue to inspire excellence and shape a future filled with promise and achievement.

Mrs. Andreina Mason Vice Principal

Garvey Maceo High School continues to be a place where students are inspired to grow, achieve, and discover their true potential. In my role as Vice Principal, I am committed to supporting a learning environment that encourages accountability, perseverance, and a genuine passion for excellence in all areas of school life.

We understand that success is a journey that requires dedication and a strong work ethic. Our students are encouraged to take ownership of their learning, remain focused on their goals, and approach every challenge with confidence and determination. Through guidance and consistent support, we help them build the resilience needed to navigate both academic and personal experiences.

Equally important is the development of character and interpersonal skills. At our school, students are given opportunities to lead, collaborate, and contribute positively to their community. Whether through academics, sports, or extracurricular involvement, we strive to create well-rounded individuals who embody respect, integrity, and a spirit of cooperation, in keeping with our motto, 'Excellence in work, study and play'.

As we continue to build on our strong foundation, we remain focused on fostering an environment where every student feels motivated and supported. Together with our dedicated staff and stakeholders, we will continue to guide our students toward meaningful success and a future filled with opportunity.
