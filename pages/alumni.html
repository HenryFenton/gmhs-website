Why Join the PSA?

Membership connects you to a lifelong network of Garvey Maceo graduates

🌐

Global Network

Connect with fellow graduates across Jamaica and internationally. Our members span industries from medicine and law to sport and the arts.

🏫

Give Back to GMHS

Support the school that shaped you — through scholarships, mentorship programmes, donations and volunteering at events.

🎓

Mentor Current Students

Share your career journey and life experience to inspire the next generation of Cubanzs preparing for life beyond GMHS.

🤝

Career Opportunities

Tap into the PSA professional network for job opportunities, business referrals and industry connections with trusted fellow alumni.

🎉

Reunions & Events

Attend annual reunions, homecoming events, sporting fixtures and cultural evenings that keep the GMHS spirit alive all year round.

🏆

Recognition & Awards

Outstanding alumni are celebrated at our annual awards, honouring achievement, service and the enduring values of Garvey Maceo.
