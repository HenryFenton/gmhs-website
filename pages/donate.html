Where Your Money Goes

Six programmes — each one making a direct difference to students at GMHS

💼

Internships

Funding work experience placements for 5th and 6th formers, giving students real-world exposure before they leave school and preparing them for the workplace.

5th & 6th Form
🎓

Scholarships

Supporting talented and deserving students with financial assistance so that the cost of education is never a barrier to achieving their full potential.

All Forms
🤝

Student Aid

Providing emergency financial support to students in need — covering essentials like school supplies, uniforms and lunch so no student is left behind.

All Forms
💻

Technology Infrastructure

Upgrading computers, internet access, tablets and digital learning tools across the school — keeping GMHS students equipped for a technology-driven world.

K–12
⚽

Sports & Extra-curricular

Funding equipment, transport, kits and entry fees for sports teams and cultural clubs — enabling students to compete, perform and excel beyond the classroom.

All Students
🛡️

COVID Relief

Supporting students and families affected by COVID-19 — from device access for online learning to welfare support for the most vulnerable in our community.
