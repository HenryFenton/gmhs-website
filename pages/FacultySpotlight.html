Teacher of the Term

Nominated by students and staff celebrating outstanding dedication and impact in the classroom

👑 Term 1 · 2026/27
FP

Mr. Floyd Palmer

💻 Information Technology Department

Mr. Palmer has served the GMHS community with distinction for over a decade, bringing a passion for technology and a genuine commitment to student achievement to every lesson. He goes above and beyond in the classroom staying after school, mentoring students through CSEC IT and creating engaging, real-world projects that bring the curriculum to life.

His students consistently achieve strong results and speak highly of his patience, creativity and high expectations for every learner.

"Mr. Palmer never gives up on any of us. He believes we can all achieve and that makes us believe it too."
— GMHS Student, Form 5
